About Us

Live News & Streaming Links was created to simplify how people access live news and trusted broadcasts online. In an age where misinformation and clickbait are widespread, we saw a need for a clean, reliable platform that only points users to official sources. Our mission is to provide a centralized gateway to real-time information without the noise, risk, or confusion often found in the modern web.

We understand how overwhelming it can be to jump from site to site or app to app just to catch a live event or news briefing. That's why we built this platform to organize, filter, and present links to live content from respected global networks. No pop-ups, no deceptive mirrors—just clean access to legitimate news and entertainment streams.

Our team is made up of media professionals, digital curators, and tech enthusiasts who believe in open access to verified information. Every link featured on our site is checked for legitimacy, functionality, and direct access. We never host pirated content or rerouted streams, because our values are rooted in transparency and ethical sharing of public resources.

Whether you’re interested in politics, sports, entertainment, or global affairs, our growing directory has you covered. From CNN and BBC to Al Jazeera and Formula 1, we aim to represent a wide range of trusted broadcasters. We routinely update our links to reflect current programming, special events, and high-demand broadcasts that matter most to our visitors.

We also believe that viewers deserve both speed and simplicity. Our minimalist design ensures that users can find what they’re looking for without distraction. With no sign-up required and no tracking, the focus remains solely on helping people get to the content they care about—safely and efficiently.

As technology continues to evolve, we are committed to growing alongside it. Our future plans include expanding into mobile app accessibility, introducing categorized filters, and offering notifications for major breaking news or scheduled events. We're listening to what our users want and working hard to meet those expectations.

Our community plays a vital role in keeping the site strong and up to date. We welcome feedback, tips, and suggestions from users around the world. Whether it's a new network to feature or a broken link to fix, your input directly improves our service and helps others benefit from a better streaming experience.

Thank you for supporting Live News & Streaming Links. We built this platform to be a trustworthy bridge between audiences and authentic media, and we’re proud to serve a growing global community. As the media landscape shifts, we remain dedicated to providing easy, ethical, and transparent access to the world’s most important live broadcasts.
